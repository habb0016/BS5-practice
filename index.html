About us

Jack Habboosh Creatives is a media creative company that offers a wide range of services to help businesses stand out in their industry. Based in Ottawa, we specialize in interactive media design and laser engraving. Our unique combination of skills and experience allows us to create custom and creative solutions for our clients, ensuring that they get exactly what they need to make their business thrive. As a student of Algonquin College, I have a strong foundation in website design and development. This knowledge allows me to understand the latest trends and technologies in the industry, and it gives me the ability to create websites that are not only visually stunning but also functional and easy to navigate. My experience in website design and development also ensures that my clients' websites are optimized for search engines and mobile devices, which is essential for any business in today's digital age. At Jack Habboosh Creatives, we understand that each business is unique and that each client has different needs. That is why we are committed to providing custom solutions for our clients, ensuring that their businesses are represented in the way they want. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and goals, and we strive to exceed their expectations. My passion for helping people express their business in the way they want sets me apart from others in the industry. I believe that every business should have a website that reflects its unique identity, and I am dedicated to making that happen for my clients. In addition to our focus on custom solutions, our location in Ottawa also allows us to offer our services to a variety of local businesses. Whether you are a small startup or a medium-sized enterprise, we have the skills and experience to help you achieve your goals. At Jack Habboosh Creatives, we are dedicated to providing our clients with high-quality services that will help their businesses stand out in the crowded market. If you are looking for a media creative company that will work with you to achieve your goals, contact us today to schedule a consultation.
